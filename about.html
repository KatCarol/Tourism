Who We Are

Nature Adventure Africa Safaris Ltd, is a premier East African travel company, with professional safari planners organizing amazing tours in Uganda, Rwanda, Kenya, Tanzania/Zanzibar, Burundi and the Democratic Republic of Congo. Our professional tour guides will bring you on the adventure of a lifetime as you discover rugged Africa in one of our safari vehicles. Whether you’re looking for a mountain gorilla or chimpanzee experience, a family holiday or travelling with friends or solo, we hope you’ll join us as we explore the shores of Victoria or Bunyonyi, trek the Rwenzori, Kilimanjaro or Elgon ranges or look for lions out on the grassy savannas of Murchison Falls National Park. Whether you’re looking for budget, mid-range, or luxury, Nature Adventure Africa Safaris is the number one service quality provider of tailored-made tours and safaris in East and Central Africa. Most importantly, we operate on the out of concern for the future of African wildlife conservation. As such, we encourage low-impact tourism, complete with small scale alternatives to mass tourism. The purpose is to educate travellers, provide funds for ecological conservation, help bolster economic development and empower local communities. We hope you’ll join in and adventure Africa with experts!
– Caroline Katrina , Director, Tour Uganda, Ltd.
